# Content Map

## Purpose

This document maps the future landing page content for the Facilitatore Tagaro MVP before any HTML, CSS, JavaScript, backend, dashboard, CRM, or implementation work begins.

The content map is based on:

- `docs/context/AI_PROJECT_CONTEXT.md`
- `docs/prd/PRD.md`
- `docs/architecture/ARCHITECTURE.md`

## 1. Hero

**Section name:** Hero

**Purpose:** Immediately explain who Vito Iacobellis is, what role he plays, and why the visitor should continue.

**Main message:** Vito Iacobellis facilitates commercial relationships and B2B opportunities between producers, distributors, buyers, and commercial partners.

**Target audience:** Buyers, distributors, importers, Ho.Re.Ca. operators, retail chains, and commercial partners.

**CTA:** Contact via WhatsApp.

**Required assets:** Professional portrait or identity image, role title, tagline, primary WhatsApp contact link.

**Notes for future implementation:** The section should be clear, direct, and credibility-focused. The first screen should communicate the role and business context without requiring the visitor to scroll.

## 2. Trust / Role

**Section name:** Trust / Role

**Purpose:** Build confidence in Vito's role as Sales & Business Development Facilitator.

**Main message:** Vito supports professional introductions, commercial relationships, and business development opportunities with a human, structured, and AI-assisted working method.

**Target audience:** Commercial stakeholders who need to understand whether the contact is credible and professionally relevant.

**CTA:** Learn about the business context.

**Required assets:** Short role description, professional bio note, optional credibility signals, domain reference `vitoiacobellis.it`.

**Notes for future implementation:** Avoid inflated claims. Focus on clarity, professional positioning, and practical commercial value.

## 3. Tagaro Business Context

**Section name:** Tagaro Business Context

**Purpose:** Explain the first represented brand and the commercial context behind the MVP.

**Main message:** Masseria Tagaro S.r.l. is an internationally-oriented winery based in Locorotondo, Puglia, Italy, and is the first business context for the platform.

**Target audience:** Buyers, distributors, importers, Ho.Re.Ca. operators, retail chains, and commercial partners evaluating wine-related opportunities.

**CTA:** Watch the presentation or review business resources.

**Required assets:** Tagaro company description, location details, brand or winery imagery, product or catalog assets when available.

**Notes for future implementation:** Make clear that Tagaro is the first represented brand, not necessarily the final scope of the platform.

## 4. What I Do

**Section name:** What I Do

**Purpose:** Explain the facilitator's practical function in simple business terms.

**Main message:** Vito helps make first contact easier, more credible, and more structured by connecting producers, distributors, buyers, and growth opportunities.

**Target audience:** Visitors who need to understand the value of the facilitation role before making contact.

**CTA:** Request a conversation.

**Required assets:** Short service description, list of facilitation activities, examples of commercial situations supported.

**Notes for future implementation:** Keep this section practical. Avoid presenting it as a broad agency or automated system.

## 5. Who I Work With

**Section name:** Who I Work With

**Purpose:** Identify the commercial audiences the platform is designed to support.

**Main message:** The platform is intended for qualified B2B contacts including buyers, distributors, importers, Ho.Re.Ca. operators, retail chains, and commercial partners.

**Target audience:** All primary B2B audience segments.

**CTA:** Select the most relevant contact path or request a conversation.

**Required assets:** Audience labels, short audience-specific descriptions, optional icons or visual markers for future implementation.

**Notes for future implementation:** The language should help each audience recognize itself quickly without creating separate complex user flows in the MVP.

## 6. Video Presentation Area

**Section name:** Video Presentation Area

**Purpose:** Provide a more human and direct way to understand Vito, Tagaro, and the commercial opportunity.

**Main message:** Watch a concise presentation to understand the role, the Tagaro context, and why a commercial conversation may be valuable.

**Target audience:** Visitors who prefer video before reading resources or making contact.

**CTA:** Watch video, then contact via WhatsApp or request a conversation.

**Required assets:** Main presentation video, thumbnail image, short video title, short supporting text.

**Notes for future implementation:** The MVP should define the video space even if the final video asset is added later. The section should not depend on backend or automation.

## 7. Resource Center

**Section name:** Resource Center

**Purpose:** Give qualified visitors access to useful business materials before contact.

**Main message:** Review practical resources to better understand the represented brand, product context, and commercial opportunity.

**Target audience:** Buyers, distributors, importers, Ho.Re.Ca. operators, retail chains, and commercial partners who need materials before a conversation.

**CTA:** Download or review resources, then request a conversation.

**Required assets:** Company profile, product catalog, commercial presentation, product sheets, certifications, or other approved business materials.

**Notes for future implementation:** Assets should be easy to replace. The MVP should keep resource organization simple and manual.

## 8. My Method

**Section name:** My Method

**Purpose:** Explain the working style behind the facilitation activity.

**Main message:** The project follows a human-led, AI-assisted, documentation-first, phase-by-phase method designed for clarity, maintainability, and responsible commercial communication.

**Target audience:** Commercial partners, strategic contacts, and stakeholders who value a structured and transparent approach.

**CTA:** Learn why to contact me or request a conversation.

**Required assets:** Method summary, communication principles, short explanation of human + AI collaboration.

**Notes for future implementation:** Emphasize that strategic decisions remain human-driven and that AI supports documentation, communication, organization, and implementation support.

## 9. Why Contact Me

**Section name:** Why Contact Me

**Purpose:** Clarify the practical reasons a qualified visitor should start a conversation.

**Main message:** Contact Vito to explore distribution, import, Ho.Re.Ca., retail, or partnership opportunities connected to Tagaro and future represented brands.

**Target audience:** Visitors who are commercially interested but need a final reason to act.

**CTA:** Contact via WhatsApp.

**Required assets:** Short benefit statements, contact prompt, optional list of suitable inquiry types.

**Notes for future implementation:** The tone should be helpful and professional, not pushy. Avoid exaggerated claims or pressure.

## 10. Digital Business Card Link

**Section name:** Digital Business Card Link

**Purpose:** Provide a compact identity and contact reference that can be shared directly.

**Main message:** Save or open Vito's digital business card for quick access to role, contact information, presentation, and resources.

**Target audience:** Visitors arriving from referrals, messages, QR codes, or professional introductions.

**CTA:** Open digital business card.

**Required assets:** Digital business card link, short identity block, contact details, optional QR code for future use.

**Notes for future implementation:** The digital business card should remain lightweight and easy to update.

## 11. Contact / Appointment Section

**Section name:** Contact / Appointment Section

**Purpose:** Provide the final conversion point for WhatsApp contact or conversation requests.

**Main message:** Qualified commercial contacts can reach Vito directly to request information, discuss opportunities, or start a professional conversation.

**Target audience:** Buyers, distributors, importers, Ho.Re.Ca. operators, retail chains, commercial partners, and strategic introducers.

**CTA:** Contact via WhatsApp or request a conversation.

**Required assets:** WhatsApp link, contact text, appointment request instructions, manual follow-up expectations.

**Notes for future implementation:** WhatsApp is the primary contact action. The MVP should not require CRM, backend, automation, or appointment scheduling integrations.

## 12. Footer

**Section name:** Footer

**Purpose:** Provide basic closing information, domain context, and lightweight navigation or reference links.

**Main message:** Facilitatore Tagaro is a professional commercial facilitation project connected to `vitoiacobellis.it`.

**Target audience:** All visitors.

**CTA:** Return to contact action or access resources.

**Required assets:** Domain reference, basic contact reference, resource link, digital business card link, optional legal or policy links if required later.

**Notes for future implementation:** Keep the footer simple. Do not use it to introduce complex systems or future platform claims.

## Content Rules

- Italian-first language
- Professional B2B tone
- No aggressive sales language
- No exaggerated claims
- Tagaro is the first represented brand, not the final platform scope
- WhatsApp is the primary contact action
- Future assets must be easy to replace
