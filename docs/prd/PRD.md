# Product Requirements Document

# Product Overview

Facilitatore Tagaro is the MVP digital presence for Vito Iacobellis in the role of Sales & Business Development Facilitator for Masseria Tagaro S.r.l., an internationally-oriented winery based in Locorotondo, Puglia, Italy.

The platform supports commercial facilitation and B2B introductions by presenting Vito's role, the Tagaro business context, relevant business resources, and direct contact paths for qualified commercial audiences.

The MVP should be simple, credible, mobile-friendly, and focused on helping buyers, distributors, importers, Ho.Re.Ca. operators, retail chains, and commercial partners understand the opportunity and request a conversation.

# Business Goal

The primary business goal is to increase credibility, facilitate commercial contact, generate qualified leads, and encourage appointment or conversation requests.

The platform should help convert interest into action by making it easy for a qualified visitor to:

- Understand who Vito Iacobellis is
- Understand the commercial context of Masseria Tagaro S.r.l.
- Watch a clear video presentation
- Access useful business resources
- Contact Vito through WhatsApp
- Request a professional conversation

# Target Audience

The target audience is composed of B2B commercial stakeholders who may be interested in wine distribution, import, retail, Ho.Re.Ca. supply, commercial partnerships, or strategic introductions.

Primary audiences include:

- Buyers
- Distributors
- Importers
- Ho.Re.Ca. operators
- Retail chains
- Commercial partners

The communication style should prioritize clarity, credibility, professionalism, simplicity, practical business value, and respect for the recipient's time.

# User Types

## Buyer

A buyer evaluates whether Masseria Tagaro products, positioning, and commercial availability may be relevant for their market, category, or purchasing needs.

## Distributor

A distributor evaluates whether Tagaro may fit their portfolio, territory, customer base, and long-term commercial strategy.

## Importer

An importer evaluates international fit, product positioning, market potential, and the practical value of opening a conversation.

## Ho.Re.Ca.

A Ho.Re.Ca. operator evaluates whether Tagaro products may be relevant for restaurants, hotels, catering, hospitality, or food service channels.

## Commercial Partner

A commercial partner evaluates possible collaboration, introductions, representation, market development, or strategic business opportunities.

# Core MVP Objectives

The MVP should:

- Establish a credible digital reference for Vito Iacobellis as Sales & Business Development Facilitator
- Present the connection with Masseria Tagaro S.r.l. clearly
- Communicate the tagline: Connecting Producers, Distributors and Growth Opportunities
- Support discovery by commercial B2B audiences
- Provide a concise path to learn about Tagaro
- Offer a video presentation area
- Provide downloadable or accessible business resources
- Make WhatsApp contact easy and visible
- Support conversation or appointment requests
- Provide basic SEO foundations for the domain `vitoiacobellis.it`
- Work well on mobile devices

# User Journeys

## Journey 1: Discover Vito Iacobellis

A visitor arrives through a direct link, search result, referral, business card, message, or introduction.

The visitor should quickly understand:

- Who Vito Iacobellis is
- His role as Sales & Business Development Facilitator
- The commercial purpose of the platform
- Why contacting him may be relevant

The desired outcome is that the visitor trusts the page enough to continue exploring.

## Journey 2: Learn about Tagaro

A visitor wants to understand the business context behind the facilitation activity.

The platform should provide a concise introduction to Masseria Tagaro S.r.l., its location in Locorotondo, Puglia, Italy, and its internationally-oriented commercial positioning.

The desired outcome is that the visitor understands why Tagaro may be relevant for distribution, import, Ho.Re.Ca., retail, or partnership opportunities.

## Journey 3: Watch video presentation

A visitor wants a faster, more human way to understand the opportunity.

The platform should provide a clear video presentation area where Vito, Tagaro, or the business opportunity can be introduced in a professional and accessible way.

The desired outcome is that the visitor gains confidence and has enough context to make contact.

## Journey 4: Download business resources

A visitor wants practical materials before deciding whether to start a conversation.

The platform should provide a resource center for business-facing materials, such as company information, product information, presentation files, catalogs, or other commercial resources when available.

The desired outcome is that qualified contacts can review useful materials independently and move toward a conversation request.

## Journey 5: Contact via WhatsApp

A visitor is ready to make quick contact.

The platform should include a clear WhatsApp contact call to action that allows the visitor to start a direct conversation with Vito.

The desired outcome is a simple, low-friction contact path for qualified B2B visitors.

## Journey 6: Request a conversation

A visitor wants to schedule or request a more structured commercial discussion.

The platform should provide a clear conversation request path, without requiring a backend, CRM, or automation in the MVP phase.

The desired outcome is that Vito receives a clear signal of interest and can continue the relationship manually.

# MVP Features

## Landing Page

A single public-facing page that presents the role, value proposition, Tagaro context, primary commercial audiences, and clear contact actions.

## Digital Business Card

A concise identity and contact area for Vito Iacobellis, including role, tagline, domain context, and direct contact options.

## Video Presentation Area

A dedicated area for one or more video presentation assets. The MVP should define the content space even if the final video asset is added later.

## Resource Center

A simple area for business resources such as catalogs, presentations, company profiles, or downloadable documents when available.

## WhatsApp Contact CTA

A prominent call to action that allows visitors to initiate contact via WhatsApp.

## SEO Foundation

Basic SEO support for discoverability, including clear page title, description, readable content structure, and business-relevant language.

## Mobile Responsive Design

The MVP should be easy to read and use on mobile devices, since many commercial contacts may access it from messages, referrals, QR codes, or mobile search.

# Success Criteria

The MVP is successful if it:

- Clearly communicates Vito Iacobellis's facilitator role
- Clearly connects the platform to Masseria Tagaro S.r.l.
- Builds credibility with B2B commercial audiences
- Makes the main contact actions easy to find
- Supports WhatsApp contact
- Supports conversation requests without complex infrastructure
- Provides a useful area for video presentation
- Provides a useful area for business resources
- Works well on mobile devices
- Avoids premature complexity
- Remains understandable and maintainable for a No-Code Developer

# Out Of Scope

The following items are explicitly out of scope for the MVP:

- CRM
- Supabase
- Dashboard
- Lead Scoring
- Relationship Capital System
- Telegram
- Automation
- AI Assistant
- Backend implementation
- Database implementation
- User accounts
- Login areas
- Admin panels
- Payment or e-commerce functionality
- Advanced analytics
- Automated appointment scheduling
- Multi-producer management
- Opportunity matching
- Trust scoring
- Ethical reciprocity scoring

# Future Evolution

The MVP should be designed as a clean first step, not as the final scope of the platform.

Future phases may explore a broader relationship and opportunity management direction after the MVP is validated and the operational need is clear.
