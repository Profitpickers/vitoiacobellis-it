# AI Project Context

## 1. Project Purpose

Facilitatore Tagaro is the documentation and MVP foundation for a commercial facilitation project connected to the domain `vitoiacobellis.it`.

The project represents the role of Sales & Business Development Facilitator, with the tagline:

**Connecting Producers, Distributors and Growth Opportunities**

The primary purpose is to support credibility, contact facilitation, lead generation, and appointment requests for business development activities connected to Masseria Tagaro S.r.l.

## 2. Current Scope

The project is currently in an MVP foundation phase only.

The present scope is limited to documentation, positioning, planning, and repository organization. The repository should remain lightweight, understandable, and maintainable.

No product interface, backend system, CRM workflow, or automation layer has been implemented at this stage.

## 3. Target Audience

The project is intended to support communication with commercial B2B audiences, including:

- Buyers
- Distributors
- Importers
- Ho.Re.Ca. operators
- Retail chains
- Commercial partners

The communication should be professional, direct, and credibility-focused, helping qualified contacts understand the business context and request a conversation or appointment.

## 4. Business Context

The primary business context is commercial facilitation and B2B introductions for Masseria Tagaro S.r.l., an internationally-oriented winery based in Locorotondo, Puglia, Italy.

The project should help present business development activity in a clear and trustworthy way, especially for international or commercially mature audiences evaluating partnership opportunities.

The role of the facilitator is to connect producers, distributors, and commercial opportunities by making first contact easier, more credible, and more structured.

## 5. Technical Constraints

The current technical phase is intentionally minimal.

Constraints for this phase:

- No backend
- No Supabase
- No CRM
- No automation
- No dashboard
- No HTML pages
- No CSS files
- No JavaScript files

The repository should remain documentation-first until implementation is explicitly approved.

## 6. Future Vision

Future phases may define a more complete digital presence for the facilitator role, potentially including structured content, contact flows, partner qualification, lead capture, and business development operations.

Any future implementation should be introduced phase by phase, after the core positioning, product requirements, architecture, and roadmap are clear.

The long-term direction is to create a practical business development support system that remains easy to operate and maintain.

## 7. Working Principles

The project should follow these working principles:

- AI-assisted execution
- Documentation-first planning
- Phase-by-phase delivery
- Clear separation between planning and implementation
- Maintainability for a No-Code Developer
- Simple, readable structure
- Conservative technical choices
- Business clarity before technical expansion

Decisions should be documented before implementation whenever they affect scope, architecture, data, operations, or commercial workflows.

## 8. Current Priorities

Current priorities are:

- Define the project context clearly
- Draft the product requirements document
- Clarify target audiences and key business messages
- Identify the most important lead and appointment request use cases
- Maintain a clean repository foundation
- Avoid premature technical implementation

The immediate next step after this document is to draft the first version of the PRD.

## 9. Out of Scope (for now)

The following items are explicitly out of scope for the current phase:

- Backend implementation
- Supabase integration
- CRM setup
- Automation workflows
- Dashboards
- Landing pages
- HTML pages
- CSS files
- JavaScript files
- Lead capture forms
- Appointment scheduling integrations
- Analytics integrations
- Payment or e-commerce functionality


## Project Owner

Vito Iacobellis is an AI-assisted Business Development Facilitator and No-Code Developer based in Northern Italy.

His role is to facilitate professional introductions, commercial relationships and business opportunities between producers, distributors, buyers, commercial operators and strategic partners.

The project is designed to combine human relationship-building skills with AI-assisted communication, documentation and business development workflows.

## Strategic Vision

Although the current MVP focuses on Masseria Tagaro S.r.l., the platform is intentionally designed to become a reusable business facilitation framework capable of supporting additional represented brands, manufacturers, distributors and commercial initiatives in the future.

Tagaro is the first implementation context, not necessarily the final scope of the platform.


## Communication Principles

All communication assets generated within this project should prioritize:

- clarity
- credibility
- professionalism
- simplicity
- respect for the recipient's time
- practical business value
- relationship building before selling

The objective is to facilitate conversations and opportunities rather than applying aggressive sales tactics.


## AI Collaboration Method

The project follows a Human + AI collaboration model.

Strategic decisions remain human-driven.

AI is used to accelerate:

- documentation
- content preparation
- communication
- organization
- implementation support

The system must remain understandable and controllable by a No-Code Developer.

## Future Strategic Concepts

The platform may evolve toward a relationship and opportunity management system capable of evaluating business opportunities, trustworthiness, reciprocity, partnership quality and long-term collaboration value across multiple represented companies and business stakeholders.

This concept is exploratory and currently out of scope for the MVP.
